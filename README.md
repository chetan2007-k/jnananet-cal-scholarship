## ğŸ¯ Problem Statement

Despite rapid digitization of government services in India, millions of students struggle to access scholarship portals due to:

- Language barriers
- Low digital literacy
- Complex application workflows
- Poor connectivity
- Confusing documentation requirements

Existing systems assume technical understanding and digital familiarity, leading to exclusion and unequal access to financial aid.

There is a need for a Cognitive Access Layer that adapts digital systems to human capability rather than forcing citizens to adapt to technology.
